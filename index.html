How It Works

Tournament Rules & Format

Everything you need to know before stepping into the match.

Team Selection

Any national or club team is allowed unless specified. No duplicate teams - first come, first served. Team changes are not permitted once the tournament begins.

Match Settings

EA FC 26 · 6-minute halves · World Class or Legendary difficulty · Injuries off · Handball off · Clear weather · Custom tactics allowed · No custom sliders.

Tournament Format

16 players: 4 groups of 4, top 2 advance to Quarters, Semis, Final. 32 players: 8 groups of 4, top 2 advance to Round of 16, Quarters, Semis, Final.

Scoring & Tiebreakers

Win = 3pts · Draw = 1pt · Loss = 0pts. Tiebreakers in order: goal difference, goals scored, head-to-head result, goals conceded, coin toss.

Knockout Rules

Knockout matches cannot end in a draw. If tied after 90 minutes: extra time is played, then penalties if still level. Winner advances immediately.

Connection

Players are responsible for a stable connection. Disconnect before 15 in-game minutes: restart. After 15 minutes: admin decides. Repeated disconnects may result in a forfeit.

Forfeits

Players have 10 minutes after being called to start. Failure to appear = 3-0 forfeit loss. Three no-shows during group play results in removal from the tournament.

Eligibility

One entry per player. Players must use their own account - sharing is prohibited. Organizers reserve the right to verify identity if needed.

Sportsmanship

No intentional disconnects, stream sniping, abusive language, racism, harassment, or cheating. Exploiting known glitches results in warnings or disqualification.
